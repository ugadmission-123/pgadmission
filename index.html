TERMS & POLICIES

  1. Applicants can register only once in the admission portal for this institution. On successful completion of registration a unique 10 digit application number is generated, which the applicant receives in SMS and Email if provided, along with his/her chosen password.
  2. The system checks whether an applicant is unique from the following data – Name, Univ. Reg. No. , University, Email and Mobile No.
  3. Applicant is responsible to provide correct information/data, to the best of his/her knowledge, while filling up the online form and in the process if it is found that there is any discrepancy, then his/her application is liable to be cancelled.
  4. After Provisional List any candidate will not able to modify any information
  5. After confirmation of the registration the candidate can take a printout of the Application Form. If a candidate wishes to apply newly due to wrong entries in previous/existing application then he/she needs to cancel the previous/existing application by generating an OTP and then do a fresh application.
  6. It is mandatory/compulsory for a candidate to provide his/her personal mobile number and Email which may be used to send various information during the process of admission.
